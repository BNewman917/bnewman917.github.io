PacMan

Animation of the classic video game character bouncing his way across the screen.

  1. Use 'Add PacMan' button to generate new PacMen on screen.
  2. Click 'Start Game' to get them all moving.
  3. Use 'Pause' and 'Unpause' to start and stop PacMen movement.

Bus Tracker

Tracks buses live using information from the Massachusetts Bay Transportation Authority on a map generated using Mapbox to show buses around the MIT and Harvard campuses in real-time.

  1. Click 'Run Tracker' in the top left corner to populate buses on the map
  2. Show/Hide bus ID by clicking on each marker
