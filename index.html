About Me

Hello! My name is Brent. I am a 32 year old Mechanical Engineering student from Ardmore, Oklahoma where I live with my wife, our daughter, two dogs and a cat. I have worked in the service industry in some capacity for most of my life. I have been a server, bartender, barista, manager, and for the past five years I have dealt table games, including at one of the largest casinos in the world. My goal is to switch to a more fulfilling career while being able to spend more time with my family.

I enjoy reading, yoga, building things, travelling, and playing video/tabletop games.
